Stop Online Loan App

Learn about your rights and how to report abusive practices.

Understanding the Issue

Many online loan apps misuse customer data and resort to harassment for repayment. This includes unauthorized access to contacts, threats, and constant calls.

What You Can Do

  • Report harassment to the cybercrime department.
  • File a complaint with your local consumer protection agency.
  • Never share sensitive personal information with unverified apps.
