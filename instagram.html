How Instagram Works

User Profile

Create a profile to share photos, videos, and connect with others.

Feed

View posts from people you follow, organized by Instagram's algorithm.

Stories

Share temporary photos and videos that disappear after 24 hours.

Explore

Discover popular and personalized content from across Instagram.

Direct Messages

Send private messages, photos, and videos to other users.

Reels

Create and watch short, entertaining videos with music and effects.

Notifications

Get alerts for new followers, likes, comments, and messages.
